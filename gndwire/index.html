GNDWIRE Records

Working with one of the most ambitious labels in Minneapolis to design and develop a unique web presence with the aim of attracting new talent. The result of this collaboration is gndwire.com.
Design, Front-End Development, Content Strategy

Started from the bottom

Founded in part by Trampled by Turtles frontman Dave Simonett, GNDWIRE Records has humble roots and an ambitious line-up of Minnesota artists. I worked with Simonett and label partners and artists to design and develop a website to showcase their newly signed lineup, including Haley Bonar and John Mark Nelson. These are the ones to watch, not to mention play on repeat.

Building for the future

GNDWIRE has big goals and even bigger potential. On the technology side, we worked with them to select a CMS and suite of tools that are simple enough to easily manage content but robust enough to grow with them as the label expands.

On the design front, this was an exciting opportunity to lead a new business through establishing a digital-forward brand. Starting with only a logo, I worked with them to explore a visual identity that they can own in both digital and traditional spaces. Photography direction, motion design, color palettes; it was all on the table.
