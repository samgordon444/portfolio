Snap Fitness

With over 2,000 locations worldwide, Snap Fitness works hard to help its members meet their goals. Their website wasn't working hard enough to support them, so we rebuilt it from the ground up.
Creative Lead, Design, Prototyping

Achieving Goals

Snap Fitness is a leader in the health and fitness lifestyle community, supporting members worldwide to achieve their health goals. As a fast-growing franchise business, they needed a digital presence that reflected their knowledge and success in fitness. I led a creative team in content production, strategy, design, and prototyping to keep the Snap Fitness brand moving toward a website that works harder for them.

Flexible, fast, and friendly

Snap Fitness trainers and owners pride themselves in the friendly, adaptable services they provide at their gyms. We wanted the site to reflect these qualities, and built both a UI and CMS that presents their content in a fast and flexible experience.
