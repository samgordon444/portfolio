Disney Research

Creating a new digital home for the happiest researchers on earth.
Creative Lead, Design

Creating for creatives

The Disney Research labs based in Los Angeles and Zurich are home to the world's most creative researchers in technology and machine sciences. This is the birthplace of Disney's most advanced animatronics, VR and AR experiences, machine-learning advancements, and all kinds of real-life magic.

Working with their in-house digital team, we built a new website with a stronger focus on their research artifacts, partnerships, and hiring efforts. Their content is fascinating and creative, so we designed some new digital brand attributes to complement the existing ones and show off their high-quality visual artifacts. With blessing from Disney corporate, we used official characters and film assets to lend credibility and recognition to the Disney Research team.
