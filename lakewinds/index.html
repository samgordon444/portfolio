Lakewinds Food Co-Op

Spreading the word about healthy, sustainable food sources in Minnesota with a fresh website design and brand extension.
Creative Lead, Design

A fresh start

Lakewinds Food Co-Op specializes in local, responsibly-sourced food and organic products. They have an eye for quality, and needed a digital partner to meet their high standards. With a recent rebranding completed by a partner agency, they were ready to strengthen the brand further with an expansion into digital.

We helped them realize their brand's potential and pushed the boundaries on their identity, from patterns and illustration to photography direction. We set them up with a content management solution that allows for greater flexibility, higher quality content and media, and showcases their vibrant brand voice more than their previous website.

Utilizing in-house talent

The Lakewinds team has a particularly useful set of skills in design and content strategy, so we got to know them individually and help them play to their strengths to support a highly visual design with a playful tone. Having social media managers and in-house designers put them a huge step forward in creating their own custom content, so we knew we could set them up for success with a unique and flexible design.
