An iconic brand delivering classic products needed a web and social media strategy partner. In addition to managing content, designing promo campaigns, and taking over their social accounts, we also had a lot of fun and took many, many photos.
Creative Lead, Design, Content Strategy, Photo & Video Direction

Smile!

Polaroid is already a household name, and for good reason. Their 80-plus year history in photo imaging brought instant film and instant memories to the world. Today they've evolved with the technology to bring us digital instant printers, 3D printing pens, and a new range of Polaroid Originals instant cameras and films.

We partnered with Polaroid to design digital assets of all kinds, manage their social media accounts, and consult on internal design projects. This engagement resulted in hundreds of photos and videos and dozens of successful social media and web campaigns.

Brand in motion

Although the majority of their products produce static images, Polaroid never stood still. Their brand has energy, and we wanted to showcase it. We explored ads, social posts, and email newsletters that utilized video and animated GIFs to show off all the creative ways their products can be used.
